MEMBERSHIP

 

MakerBar is a volunteer hacker collective with a shared space located in Hoboken, New Jersey.

We meet regularly to share knowledge, hack on projects together, teach and build community. If you’re interested in joining MakerBar, come to some of our Open Nights every Friday of the week or join the MakerBar mailing list.

 

CLASSES

 

We use our collection of knowledge and skills to teach classes in a variety of subjects from 3D Printing, Robotics, Circuit Design, Web Development; Chocolate Making! Check our calendar to learn more. Proceeds collected are donations to keep us open!

 

EVENTS

 

We offer many community events for FREE to bring community members together! From one-time open events, to repeat 'Special Interest Groups' (SIGs), learn from each other, and from the MakerBar community.  Don't be a stranger, Stop by say Hi!

Join the MakerBar mailing list, or just send us a message!
