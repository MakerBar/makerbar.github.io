Help MakerBar Succeed

We have survived for 5 years strong from donations by community members like you! It's important that we continue to raise money from donations, raffles, fundraising events, and more. We are a registered charity, so your donations may be tax deductible.

Please note we are very limited on space, so we very rarely accept used equipment for donation.

It is more important we obtain operating capital to continue strong.
